About the builder · Nº 02

Building from curiosity — where finance, business, and AI converge.

Since childhood, I have been building things — houses, models, objects — from imagination. That curiosity grew into a habit of imagining systems before creating them. Today I apply the same builder mindset to software, business systems, finance, AI products, and business strategies. I don't just learn concepts — I understand problems, research existing systems, analyse the business context, create structured documentation, and then build and iterate. I believe good products come from understanding problems before jumping to solutions.

Method · Nº 05

From problems to solutions.

+

Every stage is iterative, analytical, and research-driven — structured documentation, not guesswork.

01

Deeply understand the problem before jumping to solutions. Research existing systems, analyse the business context, and identify real needs.

02

Analyse →

Create structured documentation — PRDs, technical plans, business reports, and financial analysis — before writing a single line of code.
